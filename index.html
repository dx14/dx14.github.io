  • Undergraduate Teaching Assistant

    Data Structures and Algorithms

    Fall 2015 - Present | Durham, NC

    I am currently working as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for Duke's Data Structures and Algorithms course. My duties include leading weekly recitation classes, holding office hours for students needing help with assignments, and grading projects and exams. As a TA, I've been able to solidify my knowledge in the course topics by teaching my peers.

  • DukeEngage: Zhuhai, China

    Student Representative

    Summer 2015| Zhuhai, CN

    In summer of 2015, I traveled to Zhuhai, China as part of a DukeEngage program with a goal to promote the arts to middle school students. I spent 8 weeks at No. 9 Middle School, teaching English and pop dance classes for over 200 students. Working in a team of 16, we concluded our experience by throwing a sensitmental showcase exhibiting the students from each arts class.

  • Duke Chinese Dance

    Publicity Chair & Committee

    Fall 2013 - Present | Durham, NC

    Since my freshman year, I've been involved with Duke Chinese Dance, a dance group unifies the history of Chinese-style dance. I work as part of the Publicity Committee, which is in charge of managing social media and promoting events through digital mediums. Through this experience, I have gained important experience in working with photography and graphic design, each now an interest of mine.

  • Alpha Phi Omega Fraternity

    Brother

    Spring 2013 - Present | Durham, NC

    Pledging the spring of my freshman year, I've been a member of the Alpha Phi Omega fraternity (Lambda Nu chapter). Each semester, we engage in several service projects in the nearby Durham area. I've helped organize local charity 5K's, tutored middle school students, and even worked alongside lemurs and tigers at their shelters!
